About the Cozy Crumb Cottage

.・゜゜・ Learn more about us! ・゜゜・．

Founded by the Sugar Sisters in 1979 ~


Cozy Crumb Cottage prides itself from being a place where everyone can find a retreat and a quick treat. We pride ourselves on the experiences and tastes that we bring forth to our customers, and love making them feel good and warm with our delightful treats!


Starting from our family baking experiences, the Sugar Sisters decided to take there passions to the public because they wished to share their love for baked goods with the world. The thought of bringing a smile to their faces is what excited them, bringing forth years of rich history and commitment that they have to serving our community!
